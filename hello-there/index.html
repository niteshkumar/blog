Hello there!

Welcome to my new blog. I've been looking for the perfect place to write for a while now. It would be a joy to write on, would let me experiment with design and wouldn't own my writing. This is the closest I've gotten.

Those who know me for a while, know Parchment to be something different. For me, parchment is an excuse to share little mind explosions I have sometimes, to be creative and to say something. So when I sat down to name my blog, I thought,"what would be better than parchment"!

With this blog I will try to write more often and care a bit less about opinions. I didn't really want to serve an old dish in a new plate, so I'll start fresh on this blog. But I'll leave you with some previously written pieces of mine until I have something new to say.
